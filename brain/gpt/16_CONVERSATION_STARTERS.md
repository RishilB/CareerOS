# Conversation Starters

## Purpose

These conversation starters are designed for the CareerOS Custom GPT start screen. They should help students begin useful coaching conversations without learning commands.

## Recommended Custom GPT Starters

Use 4 starters in the Custom GPT interface:

1. Help me set up my CareerOS profile.
2. Review my resume like a recruiter.
3. I have an interview coming up. Help me prepare.
4. Analyze this job description and tell me how to tailor my profile.

## Extended Starter Library

### Career Planning

- I am confused about my career direction after MBA. Help me decide where to focus.
- Help me choose between marketing, finance, HR, operations, analytics, and consulting.
- Create a career roadmap for my target role.
- Help me understand which roles fit my strengths.

### Resume

- Review my resume like a recruiter and give me the top fixes.
- Rewrite my weakest resume bullets without exaggerating.
- Tailor my resume for this job description.
- Help me build a resume from my projects and internships.

### LinkedIn

- Review my LinkedIn profile and improve my positioning.
- Write three LinkedIn headline options for my target role.
- Rewrite my About section for recruiter visibility.
- Tell me what proof assets I should add to Featured.

### Interview

- I have an interview tomorrow. Create a focused preparation plan.
- Run a mock HR interview and give feedback after each answer.
- Help me prepare STAR stories from my experience.
- Prepare me for behavioral interview questions.

### Company Research

- Help me research this company before my interview.
- Create a company brief with likely interview themes.
- Tell me what smart questions I should ask the interviewer.
- Compare this company with its competitors for interview preparation.

### Job Description Analysis

- Analyze this job description and identify the must-have skills.
- Compare my resume with this job description.
- Tell me what keywords I should naturally include.
- Create likely interview questions from this JD.

### Learning and Skill Gaps

- Identify my skill gaps for this role.
- Create a 30-day learning roadmap for business analytics.
- Help me build a project that can strengthen my resume.
- Tell me what I should learn this week for placements.

### Networking

- Write a thoughtful alumni outreach message.
- Help me ask for an informational interview.
- Draft a follow-up message after a networking call.
- Help me ask for a referral professionally.

### Communication

- Rewrite this email professionally.
- Help me create a 60-second self-introduction.
- Prepare me for a group discussion.
- Help me communicate salary expectations professionally.

### Placement Preparation

- Score my placement readiness.
- Build a 30-day placement preparation plan.
- Help me organize my application process.
- Create a weekly career review for me.

## Starter Design Principles

Good starters should:

- Be action-oriented.
- Signal a clear workflow.
- Match common student anxieties.
- Encourage asset sharing.
- Avoid vague prompts like "What can you do?"
