# Interview Evaluation Rubric

## Purpose

This rubric defines how CareerOS should evaluate interview answers and mock interview performance.

## Scoring Method

Score answers out of 100 when useful.

| Area | Points |
| --- | ---: |
| Relevance to question | 15 |
| Structure | 15 |
| Evidence quality | 20 |
| Role and company alignment | 15 |
| Communication clarity | 15 |
| Self-awareness | 10 |
| Concision and delivery | 10 |
| Total | 100 |

## Relevance: 15 Points

Strong:

- Directly answers the question.
- Uses relevant example.
- Avoids unrelated detail.

Weak:

- Avoids question.
- Gives generic answer.
- Uses mismatched story.

## Structure: 15 Points

Strong:

- Clear beginning, middle, end.
- Uses STAR, CAR, or PREP naturally.
- Easy to follow.

Weak:

- Rambling.
- No clear result.
- Jumps between points.

## Evidence Quality: 20 Points

Strong:

- Specific situation.
- Clear personal contribution.
- credible outcome.
- measurable or scoped proof.

Weak:

- Vague claims.
- Team outcome without personal role.
- No result.
- inflated ownership.

## Role and Company Alignment: 15 Points

Strong:

- Connects answer to target role.
- Shows understanding of company or function.
- Demonstrates relevant competency.

Weak:

- Good story but no role connection.
- Generic motivation.
- No company-specific reason.

## Communication Clarity: 15 Points

Strong:

- Plain language.
- confident tone.
- logical flow.
- professional vocabulary.

Weak:

- Too much filler.
- unclear wording.
- defensive tone.
- overly memorized.

## Self-Awareness: 10 Points

Strong:

- Reflects on learning.
- acknowledges limitations honestly.
- shows growth.

Weak:

- Blames others.
- denies weaknesses.
- cannot explain learning.

## Concision and Delivery: 10 Points

Strong:

- Appropriate length.
- no unnecessary detail.
- calm pacing.

Weak:

- Too long.
- too short.
- rushed or unfocused.

## Score Interpretation

| Score | Interpretation |
| --- | --- |
| 85-100 | Strong answer; polish and practice follow-ups. |
| 70-84 | Good answer; needs sharper evidence or delivery. |
| 55-69 | Partially effective; needs restructuring. |
| 40-54 | Weak answer; rebuild with stronger story. |
| Below 40 | Not interview-ready for this question. |

## Feedback Format

```markdown
## Interview Feedback
Score: __ / 100

### What Worked

### What Needs Improvement

### Better Structure

### Stronger Version

### Follow-Up Question

### Practice Task
```

## Common Interview Red Flags

- Cannot explain resume bullet.
- Uses fake or exaggerated experience.
- Gives no company-specific motivation.
- Talks negatively about previous people or institutions.
- Uses vague strengths.
- Cannot explain failure or weakness maturely.
- Sounds memorized.

## Mock Interview Behavior

CareerOS should:

- Ask one question at a time.
- Wait for response.
- Score or diagnose.
- Give concise feedback.
- Ask a realistic follow-up.
- Track recurring issues.
